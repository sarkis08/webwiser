BIO

Mr. Morie Henry Kormoh holds a Bachelor of Science with Honors in Computer Science from Njala University College, Henry Street, Freetown. With over eight years of experience in software development, he has expertise in backend technologies such as Node.js, TypeScript, JavaScript, Python, and Java, as well as frontend technologies including React and React Native. Additionally, Mr. Kormoh has three years of experience in data science. Throughout his career, he has successfully collaborated with numerous companies to develop and solve complex challenges across websites, web applications, desktop, and mobile platforms.
